I think the first time I attempted this I made a mistake in problem 1(b). Looking over my notes, it seems like I was still confused about the definition of idempotent. Rather than coming up with a endomap \(e\) that satisfied \(e \circ e = e\text{,}\) I seem to have constructed one where \(e \circ e = 1_A\text{.}\) As a result of using an involution instead, I was justifiably having a hard time showing the properties in part (c). This defintion actually appears on the page adjacent to the test with a note to "see Article III", so I'd expect that this was a common mistake made by students while in development.
